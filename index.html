Common Questions

What Austin homeowners ask before they call.

Straight answers. No upsells. If your question isn't here, call and we'll walk you through it before we ever quote.

Do you charge for estimates?

No. On-site diagnostic and written flat-rate quote are free for residential calls in our service area. You approve the price before any work starts.

How fast can you actually be here?

15-minute callback, same-day for non-emergency. For live wires, sparking outlets, or panel failures we dispatch immediately, day or night — that's our guarantee.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Texas Master Electrician licensed, fully insured for residential and commercial work, and every job is permitted and inspected when code requires it.

What does a typical service call cost?

Simple repairs (outlets, switches, breakers) start around $150–$250 flat. Panel upgrades, EV chargers, and rewires are quoted on-site after we look at your system. You'll know the number before we touch a wire.
